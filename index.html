Hi, I’m Bonnie Roe, a professional drummer and drum teacher, located in Mernda. Whether you're just starting out or looking to level up your skills, I’m here to help you.

My Drumming Vibe

I play in a bass and drum duo called Palpitations, where I combine drumming on my kit with with drumming on my drum synth. We incorporate rock, funk and metal into our music.
You will notice when you hear me play that I am heavily influenced by heavy rock but play with a funk feel.

Technique
Build a strong foundation so you can play fast and flashy
Feel
Learn how to incorporate 'feel' into your playing and get your audience to connect to your playing
Creativity
I’ll teach you creativity on the drums and how to improvise
Performance
Get tips on playing live and with other musicians (it doesn’t need to be scary)
